# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We commit to making participation in Pet Halo welcoming, respectful, and harassment-free for everyone, regardless of background, identity, experience, or ability.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful, specific, and constructive.
- Critique ideas and changes, not people.
- Protect other users' privacy and never pressure anyone to share private Codex data or screenshots.
- Accept maintainers' decisions about safety, compatibility, scope, and release gates.
- Report security and privacy issues through the private process in `SECURITY.md`.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, discrimination, threats, sexualized conduct, doxxing, publication of private data, deliberate disruption, or repeated bad-faith interaction is not acceptable in project spaces.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit or remove contributions and restrict participation when behavior violates this policy. Reports should be made privately through the repository's available maintainer or security contact. Retaliation against reporters is prohibited.

This policy applies in repository discussions, reviews, issues, release channels, and other spaces where someone represents the project.
